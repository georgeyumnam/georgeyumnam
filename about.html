  • I qualified the National Talent Search Examination (NTSE) 2008. The National Talent Search Examination was conducted by the National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT) with the aim of selecting 500 bright students from all over the country. The evaluation scheme for the qualification was divided into 3 tiers, where the candidate is initially required to qualify for a state level written examination followed by a national level written examination. Finally, the candidate has to appear for the interview which checks the candidate's understanding of the subject, awareness of current-affairs, ability to apply assimilated knowledge, adaptability to a situation, etc. I was one among the 5 students selected for the NTSE scholarship program from the state of Manipur. Thereafter, I continued to receive a scholarship of ₹ 6,000 annually.
